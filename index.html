What We Do

We lovingly raise adorable Bernedoodle puppies in the Sonoran Desert of Arizona. While we are based in Mesa, Arizona, and focus mostly on serving the western states - like Arizona, California, Oregon, Nevada, Washington, Utah, Idaho, Colorado and New Mexico, we can transport or flight nanny our puppies, with a trusted member of our team, throughout the United States.

Our current breeding focus is Standard F1 Bernedoodles, with Nali and Axel. Both parent dogs are on site, as they are beloved members of our family. Due to inheriting one poodle furnishing gene, this generation of Bernedoodles does offer less shedding than a typical Bernese Mountain Dog, but it is not a true hypoallergenic coat. F1's can have a flat or wavy coat, a slight curl, or a tight poodle curl, and will require grooming.
